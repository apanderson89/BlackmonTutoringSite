About Me

Hello my name is Jordan Blackmon. I have numerous years of tutoring experience. I have tutored college kids as well as K-12 in a various amount of subjects and test prep courses. With that being said, I have a bachelors from Houston Baptist University and masters degree from William Carey University. My specialist degree is in Interscholastic education where I obtained the ability to work with kids who have dyslexia, ADHD and things of that nature. I got into tutoring because I wanted to help out students who are going through the same struggles I had growing up. I never really enjoyed school and when I needed help I would never ask, because it was just not fun. As I got older I appreciated school a lot more and began to excel. Earning Magna Cum Laude throughout college. I started Blackmon Tutoring because I wanted to inspire the youth and make learning fun. My goal is to use my expertise to impact students life’s and change their views on education in a positive manner.
